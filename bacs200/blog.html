Response to the COVID-19 Pandemic

The Covid-19 pandemic has effected the human race as a whole. In this blog the pros/cons of the response to the pandemic will be discussed. While the majority will view the pandemic as a negative event (and rightfully so) there are positives that can be seen as a result of this devastating event in human history.

Social Implications

The pandemic plagued not only our health but our sociability. As a response to the pandemic, we were told to stay indoors much of the time and to keep our faces covered. This limited how we were able to interact and for most in a negative manner. A positive was the reaction of tech companies, they created new or more improved video conferencing technology to aid in that interactionn (ex. Zoom).
